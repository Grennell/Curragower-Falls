● How to read the charts

Conditions Guide


🌊 Ball's Bridge River Level

< 0.75m Release too low. No wave.
0.75 – 0.88m Low & shallow. Left foam pile, right side green.
0.88 – 1.00m Full foam pile. A bit flushy. Good level.
1.00 – 1.12m Full foam pile. Flat & thrasy, more like a big hole.
1.12 – 1.24m Full foam pile. Good level.
1.24 – 1.37m ⭐ Full foam pile with green. Best level.
1.37 – 1.49m High but good. Full foam, bit of green, getting flushy.
1.49 – 1.73m High. Foam in and out. Great for surf kayaks / half slices.
1.73 – 1.98m High. Gower green. Lil' Gower foam pile forming.
> 1.98m Flood. Biggie smalls potentially running.

🌊 Tide Level at Limerick Dock

< 1.0m Tides are good.
1.0 – 1.15m Tides on the verge of being too high.
> 1.15m Tides too high. Wave unlikely to form.

ℹ️ About this data

River level data comes from the OPW gauging station at Ball's Bridge (station 25061), sampled at the moment of each low tide. Tide predictions are from tidetimes.org.uk for Limerick Dock. Data is updated automatically via Google Sheets.
